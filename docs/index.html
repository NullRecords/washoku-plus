What “Washoku” means here

Inspired by a pattern, not a costume.

Washoku refers to traditional Japanese food culture: seasonal ingredients, rice, fish, vegetables, soup, balance, and respect for the meal.

Washoku Plus uses that structure as a starting point, then adapts it across Mexican, Mediterranean, Korean, vegetarian, and everyday meals.
