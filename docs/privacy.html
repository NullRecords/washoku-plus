Privacy Policy

Washoku Plus is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What We Collect

  • Meal logs, preferences, and health-related notes you choose to enter in the app
  • Anonymous usage data to improve the app

How We Use Data

  • To provide and improve the Washoku Plus service
  • To help you track your own meal patterns and health insights

Health Data

Any health-related data you enter is stored securely and used only to provide you with personal insights. We do not share your health data with third parties except as required by law or with your explicit consent.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as analytics or email providers) that process data on our behalf. These providers are required to protect your information and may not use it for other purposes.

Your Choices

  • You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Contact
